### The Legendary Solars

I wasn't able to find the cover i cooked up for this particular story, but did manage to recover some pages from this one too!

This story starts off with a teenage group in some unnamed school, meeting up in the morning. This is a modern day (? not right now per say, a couple decades after this century, but not wrecked enough to call it dystopian exactly)

Then there's your stereotypical evi organisation which is conducting unknown experiments on humans to give them superpowers for their unknown cause, and they come to the school to test on kids, except our favourite local group creates their own rebel group and fight their way out of the school

Only to end up in the clutches of that evil organisation, and get pumped with mysterious chemicals

### Webnovel incident
soo... some representatives from the official webnovel acocunt reached out to me via a personal message on Wattpad, that month when Legendary Solars hit #1 on #save-the-earth for a straight month.

Webnovel was a novel writing and reading platform launched in 2017, so around 2018 when I was contacted, they were still a relatively not so well-known website for stories. Atleast I wasn't too informed about them back then
They said they really liekd that story, and would be interested in signing me up for a small paid creators program on their site! If interested, reach out to their official email asking for a representative

I actually did text them, and they reached out to me too, explaining the creators program, which now that I read, was actually really nice starting out. The question now is, what did I say to them.

Nothing. My 9th grader ass ghosted them, and their second follow up mail too, because of exams and the fact that I forgot to check my emails regularly and just kept posting on Wattpad. I would have slapped my 14 year old self so hard like oh god that was such a good oppurtunity for me man why the fuck did i fumble that so badly oh GOD
